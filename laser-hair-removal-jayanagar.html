How Does it Feel? The Experience

Many patients are surprised by how comfortable the treatment is. Unlike older-generation lasers that felt like painful hot stings, our technology at Dermasculpt features a dynamic cooling system. As the laser targets the hair follicle, a burst of cold air or a sapphire cooling tip touches the skin simultaneously. Most describe the sensation as a "light flick" or a quick "cool snap" that vanishes instantly.

There is no recovery time. You can walk into our Jayanagar clinic during your lunch break and return to work immediately after. Your skin might have a slight pinkish glow for an hour — a positive sign that the follicles have successfully absorbed the energy.

The 3-Step Technology Advantage

We use a Triple-Wavelength System that targets three different depths of the hair root simultaneously:

  • 755nm (Alexandrite): Ideal for finer, lighter hair on the face or arms.
  • 810nm (Diode): Deep penetration for large areas like legs and back.
  • 1064nm (Nd:YAG): Specifically tuned for darker Indian skin tones — the laser targets only the hair, not the skin.

Treatment Journey & Aftercare

We recommend 6 to 8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart to catch every hair in its active growth phase. After each session:

  • ✓
    Sun Protection: Apply SPF 30+ to the treated area for a few days.
  • ✓
    Stay Cool: Avoid hot saunas, steam, or heavy workouts for 24–48 hours.
  • ✓
    No Plucking: Avoid waxing between sessions — shaving is perfectly fine.

Laser Hair Removal FAQs

How many sessions of laser hair reduction are needed?

Most patients need 6-8 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for permanent results. At Dermasculpt Jayanagar, we assess your skin and hair type to recommend the exact session count.

Is laser hair removal safe for PCOS?

Yes, laser hair removal is highly effective for PCOS-related hair growth. While it significantly reduces density, additional maintenance sessions may be needed due to hormonal influences.

What is the cost of laser hair removal in Bangalore?

Prices at Dermasculpt Clinic start from ₹1,500 per session depending on the area. We offer attractive full-body and multi-session packages in Jayanagar.

Can I shave between sessions?

Yes, you should only shave between sessions. Avoid waxing or plucking as the hair follicle must be intact for the laser to target it effectively.
