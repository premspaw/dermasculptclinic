What is Hair Transplant surgery? Hair transplant is a micro-surgical procedure that involves moving hair follicles from a 'donor site' (usually the back of the head) to a 'recipient site' (the thinning or bald areas). At Dermasculpt Clinic in Jayanagar, Bangalore, led by expert dermatologist Dr. Deepak Devkar, we specialize in the advanced FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) technique. This method ensures a natural-looking hairline, maximum graft survival, and a virtually painless experience with no visible linear scarring. Our dedicated surgical team focuses on precision and density to restore not just your hair, but your confidence as well.

Hair Transplant FAQs

What is FUE hair transplant?

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) is a minimally invasive hair restoration technique where individual hair follicles are extracted from the donor area and implanted into the thinning regions.

Is hair transplant permanent?

Yes, the transplanted hair is harvested from the permanent zone (back of the head) and is genetically resistant to thinning, providing lifelong results.

How much does a hair transplant cost in Jayanagar?

The cost depends on the number of grafts required. At Dermasculpt, we offer transparent pricing starting from ₹45,000, with detailed consultations by Dr. Devkar.

How long is the recovery?

Most patients can return to work within 3-5 days. The transplanted hair starts growing after 3 months, with full results visible in 9-12 months.
