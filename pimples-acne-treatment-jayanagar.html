Understanding Your Acne

Acne is not just a "teenage problem." Hormonal acne, stress-induced breakouts, and adult cystic acne are increasingly common in Bangalore's urban lifestyle. At Dermasculpt, we begin with a thorough skin analysis to understand the root cause — whether it's excess sebum, clogged follicles, bacteria, or hormonal imbalance — before recommending a protocol.

How Our Treatment Feels

Many patients expect chemical peels to be harsh or uncomfortable, but our formulations are calibrated for your skin's tolerance. A light AHA peel feels like a mild tingling sensation — similar to a cool, fizzy mask — that clears within minutes. LED light therapy is completely painless, like sitting in front of a warm, coloured lamp for 20 minutes. Most patients describe leaving their session with skin that already feels smoother and tighter.

There's virtually no downtime for most treatments. You may experience slight redness or peeling for 1-2 days after a deeper peel — which is actually the skin renewing itself. It's a great sign.
