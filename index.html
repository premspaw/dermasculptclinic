Common Questions

Skin & Hair Care FAQs

What is the best treatment for permanent hair reduction?

The best treatment for permanent hair reduction is Advanced Laser Hair Removal using triple-wavelength technology (Alexandrite, Diode, and YAG). This combination targets hair follicles at different depths, ensuring safe and effective results for all skin types in Bangalore.

How many sessions are needed for PRP hair loss treatment?

For most patients experiencing hair thinning, we typically recommend a series of 4 to 6 sessions of PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) or GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) therapy, spaced 4 weeks apart, to see significant natural follicle regrowth.

PRP vs GFC: Which is better for hair regrowth?

GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) is generally superior as it is a more advanced, purer form of PRP. It delivers up to 5 times more active growth factors directly to the hair follicles, often yielding faster and more visible density improvements compared to standard PRP.

Is Morpheus8 effective for skin tightening?

Morpheus8 is highly effective for skin tightening and remodeling. It combines microneedling with fractional radiofrequency (RF) energy to stimulate collagen production deep in the dermis, reducing skin laxity and improving texture with minimal downtime.

Is Laser Hair Removal safe for Indian skin tones?

Yes, it is completely safe. At Dermasculpt, we use the Long-Pulse Nd:YAG laser, which is specifically designed to safely bypass the melanin in darker Indian skin tones and target only the hair follicle, eliminating risks of burns or pigmentation.

What is the best treatment for acne scars in Jayanagar?

The most advanced treatments for deep acne scars are Fractional CO2 Laser and Morpheus8 RF. These procedures trigger the skin's natural healing process to fill in 'pitted' scars and smooth out the skin surface effectively.
