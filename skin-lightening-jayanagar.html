Achieving Your Best Glow

Skin lightening at Dermasculpt is not about changing your natural beauty, but about correcting imperfections like sun damage, melasma, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. Our treatments focus on inhibiting melanin production and promoting cell turnover to reveal fresher, more vibrant skin.

How Our Treatment Feels

Most of our brightening procedures are designed for maximum comfort. Chemical peels might cause a light tingling, while Laser Toning feels like a warm snap against the skin. Glutathione and Vitamin C IV therapies are simple intravenous sessions that allow you to relax while your skin receives essential antioxidants. Most patients experience zero to minimal downtime, making these treatments perfect for those with busy Bangalore lifestyles.
