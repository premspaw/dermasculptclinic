Botox &
Dermal Fillers

What is the difference between Botox and Dermal Fillers? Botox and dermal fillers are both minimally invasive injectable treatments used for facial rejuvenation, but they work differently. Botox is a neuromodulator that temporarily relaxes the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles like crow's feet and forehead lines. Dermal fillers, typically made of hyaluronic acid, are used to restore lost volume, smooth deep folds, and enhance facial contours such as the cheeks, lips, and jawline. At Dermasculpt Clinic in Jayanagar, Bangalore, Dr. Deepak Devkar provides personalized aesthetic consultations to determine the optimal combination of these treatments for a natural, well-rested appearance with minimal downtime.
