Precision Laser Removal

Moles are common skin growths, but when they are unwanted or located in prominent areas, laser removal is the most effective and aesthetic solution. Our CO₂ laser allows for extreme precision, vaporizing the mole tissue layer by layer until it is flush with the skin, promoting natural healing with virtually no trace left behind.

What to Expect

The procedure is straightforward and fast. After a quick consultation and skin analysis by Dr. Devkar, the area is numbed with a local anesthetic. The laser treatment itself takes only a few minutes. You may have a small scab for a few days, which heals quickly to reveal smooth, clear skin underneath. There is no need for stitches or complex aftercare.
