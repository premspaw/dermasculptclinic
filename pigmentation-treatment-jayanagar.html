Science of Skin Clarity

Pigmentation is often deep-seated and requires a systematic approach. At Dermasculpt, we don't believe in "one size fits all." We analyze the depth and type of your pigmentation — whether it's epidermal (surface) or dermal (deep) — and create a protocol that targets the melanin-producing cells directly while protecting the surrounding skin.

Personalized Protocols

Our treatment plans often combine multiple modalities for the best results. We might use chemical peels to exfoliate surface pigment, followed by laser toning to break down deeper melanin. This multi-layered strategy ensures that we address the root cause and provide long-lasting clarity.

Pigmentation FAQs

How long does it take to see results for melasma?

Melasma is a chronic condition, but with our laser toning and medical peels, most patients see significant lightening within 3-4 months of consistent treatment.

Are chemical peels safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, we use customized AHA and Glow peels that are specifically chosen based on your skin's sensitivity to ensure brightening without irritation.

Will the pigmentation return after treatment?

Maintenance is key. We provide a customized post-treatment skincare regimen, including high-quality sun protection, to prevent recurrence.

What is Carbon Laser Peel (Hollywood Peel)?

It's a quick, painless procedure that uses a layer of liquid carbon and laser energy to exfoliate the skin, clear pores, and provide an instant glow.
