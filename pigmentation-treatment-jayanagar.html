Science of Skin Clarity

Pigmentation is often deep-seated and requires a systematic approach. At Dermasculpt, we don't believe in "one size fits all." We analyze the depth and type of your pigmentation — whether it's epidermal (surface) or dermal (deep) — and create a protocol that targets the melanin-producing cells directly while protecting the surrounding skin.

Personalized Protocols

Our treatment plans often combine multiple modalities for the best results. We might use chemical peels to exfoliate surface pigment, followed by laser toning to break down deeper melanin. This multi-layered strategy ensures that we address the root cause and provide long-lasting clarity.
